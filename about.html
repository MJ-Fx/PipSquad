The PipSquad Story

After years trading for institutional clients and witnessing firsthand how the "smart money" manipulates price action, I became frustrated seeing retail traders consistently on the wrong side of the market.

In 2018, I began documenting these institutional strategies and teaching them to a small group of traders. The results were transformative - students who had struggled for years suddenly began seeing consistent profitability.

PipSquad was born from this simple premise: what if every retail trader had access to the same knowledge as institutional traders?
